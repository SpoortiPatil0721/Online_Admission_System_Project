About College

The Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) program at KLE Society commenced in 2013 under the leadership of Prof. G.S. Sheeri, who served as the Incharge Principal for both BBA and BCA courses. Initially, the program operated from KLE Society's Shri Mrityunjay College of Arts and Commerce campus in Dharwad. In August 2015, the BCA program transitioned to the RLS College campus, Dharwad, offering students access to modern, fully equipped classrooms, a comprehensive library, and advanced computer labs.

The department prides itself on having a team of highly qualified faculty members and robust hardware and software resources. At KLE Society's BCA program, education is viewed as more than just acquiring qualifications; it focuses on imparting quality knowledge that shapes the character and future of students.

Affiliated with Karnataka University and Accredited by AICTE, the program strictly adheres to the university's syllabus and curriculum under National Education Policy (NEP).
