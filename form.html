General Instructions for BCA Admissions:

← Back to Dashboard
1. Eligibility Criteria
  1. Candidates must have completed 10+2 / PUC / equivalent from a recognized board.
  2. The applicant should have studied Mathematics / Computer Science / Information Practice as one of the subjects (preferred but not mandatory for some colleges).
  3. A minimum aggregate of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/Cat-I, if applicable) in qualifying exams is required.
2. Application Process
  1. Applications must be submitted online through the college admission portal.
  2. All fields marked with * are mandatory. Incomplete applications will be rejected.
  3. Valid contact details (email and mobile number) must be provided for future communication.
3. Documents Required (at the time of admission)
  1. SSLC/10th Marks Card (original + 2 copies).
  2. PUC/12th Marks Card (original + 2 copies)
  3. Transfer Certificate (TC)
  4. Conduct Certificate
  5. Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  6. Passport-size photographs (4â€“6 copies)
  7. Aadhaar card copy
  8. Migration Certificate (for out-of-state candidates)
  9. Income Certificate (if applying under fee concession schemes)
4. Fee Structure
  1. Admission will be confirmed only after the payment of prescribed fees.
  2. Detailed fee structure is available on the college website or admission portal.
  3. Fees once paid are non-refundable, except under special cases as per college refund policy.

Note: As per the above instructions students must qualify and provide every document mentioned.
